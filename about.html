About Holy Spirit Catholic Church

A growing, thriving Catholic community on Johns Island — rooted in faith, committed to one another, and open to all.

Who We Are

Our Mission

Holy Spirit Catholic Church exists to help every person encounter the living God — and to grow as a community of disciples who worship, learn, and serve together.

We believe that God has more for every person than they have yet received — more joy, more peace, more purpose, more love. Our parish life is built around three core commitments: Discover — encountering God through worship and the sacraments; Develop — growing in faith at every age and stage of life; and Deliver — pouring our gifts into service to our neighbors and our world.

We are part of the Diocese of Charleston, and we are proud to be the Catholic community serving Johns Island and the surrounding coastal communities of South Carolina.

Our Story

Our History

Holy Spirit Catholic Church has been a place of faith, community, and welcome for the people of Johns Island for many years.

Johns Island is one of the oldest and most historically rich sea islands on the South Carolina coast — and Holy Spirit has been part of its story for generations. What began as a small faith community has grown into a vibrant parish, welcoming families, retirees, longtime residents, and newcomers from across the Lowcountry and beyond.

Over the years, our parish has grown not only in numbers but in depth — expanding our worship space, our formation programs, and our commitment to serving our neighbors. We are proud of our roots and grateful for every person who has called Holy Spirit home.
