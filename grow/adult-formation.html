Adult Faith Formation

Growing in faith is not something you outgrow. There is always more — and it is always worth pursuing.

Our adult formation programs are designed to meet you where you are — whether you are new to the Catholic faith, a lifelong Catholic hungry to go deeper, or somewhere in between. Come curious. Come as you are.

Programs & Opportunities

Formation for Adults

Bible Study

Explore the living Word of God in a welcoming, discussion-based setting. Our Bible study groups meet regularly throughout the year to walk through both the Old and New Testament, drawing out the richness of Scripture and applying it to everyday life.

Open to: All adults — no prior biblical knowledge required.

Learn More

Retreats

Step away from the noise and encounter God in the quiet. We offer men's retreats, women's retreats, and couples retreats throughout the year — times set apart for prayer, reflection, and renewal that participants consistently describe as life-changing.

Open to: All adults. Specific retreats for men, women, and couples.

View Upcoming Retreats

Cursillo

Cursillo — meaning "short course in Christianity" — is a powerful three-day experience designed to help participants discover and deepen their relationship with Christ and the Church. Many people describe their Cursillo weekend as a turning point in their faith life.

Open to: All adult Catholics.

Ask Us About Cursillo

Life in the Spirit Seminar

The Life in the Spirit Seminar is a seven-week journey designed to help Catholics discover or rediscover the gifts of the Holy Spirit active in their lives. It is an invitation to experience the living God in a fresh and tangible way.

Open to: All adult Catholics seeking deeper encounter with the Holy Spirit.

Learn More

Alpha

Alpha is a series of interactive sessions exploring life, faith, and meaning. Each session involves a short talk followed by an open, judgment-free conversation about the big questions of life. It is welcoming to people of all backgrounds — Catholic, non-Catholic, skeptical, or simply curious.

Open to: Anyone — Catholic or not. Perfect for inviting friends and neighbors.

Find the Next Alpha

Book Club & Small Groups

Small groups are at the heart of how we grow together as a parish. Whether it is a faith-based book club, a neighborhood small group, or a seasonal study, these intimate settings foster deep friendship, accountability, and spiritual growth.

Open to: All adults. New groups form seasonally.

Join a Group

Online Formation Resources

Not able to attend in person? We recommend several outstanding online formation resources including formed.org, the USCCB Bible app, and the Hallow prayer app. Ask us for access details and recommendations tailored to where you are in your journey.

Open to: All parishioners.

Come and See

None of these programs require any prior knowledge, any particular level of faith, or any long-term commitment to try. The only requirement is a willingness to show up.
