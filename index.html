A Word from Our Pastor
Welcome to Holy Spirit Catholic Church. It is a great blessing to have you join us. Whether you are new to our community, a longtime parishioner, or exploring the Catholic faith for the very first time, we welcome and embrace you wholeheartedly. We want to walk with you in your journey with Christ.

Our parish exists to help every person encounter the living God — in the sacraments, in community, and in service to one another. You were made for more than you may realize, and we believe God is inviting you into something beautiful here. I am grateful you are here, and I hope Holy Spirit Catholic Church feels like home.

Father Stephen Dyas

Pastor, Holy Spirit Catholic Church

How We Grow Together

God Has More for You

Our parish life is built around three simple invitations. Wherever you are in your journey, there is a place for you here.

Discover More

Encounter God through the sacraments, Mass, adoration, and the rich prayer life of our parish community. Every sacrament is an invitation to go deeper with God.

Worship

Develop More

Deepen your faith through Bible study, retreats, adult formation, OCIA, children's religious education, and youth ministry. There is formation here for every age and every stage of life.

Grow

Deliver More

Put your time, talent, and treasure to work — serving our neighbors, strengthening our community, and sharing the love of Christ with everyone we encounter.

Serve
Life in Abundance

There Is Always More

You were not created to just survive life — you were created for life in abundance. At Holy Spirit Catholic Church you will find a growing, thriving Catholic community who embrace the transformative power of Christ working through the Holy Spirit to deepen their faith while welcoming and serving their neighbors.

Stories of More

What Happens When People Open Themselves to Receiving More

These are the stories of ordinary people who said yes — and found something extraordinary waiting for them.

I moved to Johns Island not knowing anyone. I walked into Holy Spirit Catholic Church on a Sunday morning not sure what to expect. Within a month I had friends, a small group, and a sense of belonging I had not felt in years.

Maria T.

Parishioner since 2022

I had been away from the Church for almost fifteen years. Coming back felt impossible — until I walked through these doors. No judgment, just welcome. I received the Sacrament of Reconciliation for the first time in a long time and it changed my life.

James R.

Returned Catholic

I came to OCIA with a thousand questions and zero expectations. What I found was not a class — it was an encounter with God. I was baptized at the Easter Vigil and I have never been the same.

Sarah M.

Received into the Church, Easter 2024
